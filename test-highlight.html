How to Test the Highlight Feature

  1. Install the Personal Summarizer extension in Chrome
  2. Select any text on this page
  3. Right-click and choose "Highlight Text"
  4. Choose a color (Yellow, Blue, Green, or Pink)
  5. Click on the highlighted text to add a comment
  6. Reload the page - your highlights should persist!
  7. Click the extension icon and go to "View My Highlights"

Features to Test

  • Highlight text with different colors
  • Add comments to highlights
  • Edit existing comments
  • Delete individual highlights
  • Clear all highlights
  • Persistence across page reloads

Sample Article: The Future of AI

Artificial Intelligence has transformed the way we interact with technology. From voice assistants to recommendation systems, AI is everywhere in our daily lives. This technology is not just about automation; it's about augmenting human capabilities and making our interactions with machines more natural and intuitive.

Machine Learning, a subset of AI, enables systems to learn from data without being explicitly programmed. This has led to breakthroughs in image recognition, natural language processing, and predictive analytics. Companies across industries are leveraging machine learning to gain insights from their data and improve decision-making.

The ethical implications of AI are also becoming increasingly important. As these systems become more powerful, questions about privacy, bias, and accountability come to the forefront. Researchers and policymakers are working together to ensure that AI development is aligned with human values and societal well-being.

Another Test Section: Climate Change

Climate change represents one of the most pressing challenges of our time. Rising global temperatures, extreme weather events, and melting ice caps are clear indicators that our planet's climate is changing at an unprecedented rate.

Scientists emphasize that immediate action is required to mitigate the worst effects of climate change. This includes transitioning to renewable energy sources, protecting forests, and developing sustainable agricultural practices. Individual actions, such as reducing waste and choosing eco-friendly products, also play a crucial role.

International cooperation is essential for addressing climate change effectively. The Paris Agreement represents a global commitment to limit temperature increases and support vulnerable nations in adapting to climate impacts. However, achieving these goals requires sustained effort and political will from all countries.
